Frequently asked questions

Common questions before booking a garage inspection.

Ask QSV
Do you do engine rebuilds?

Yes, BMW N47/N57 and JLR Ingenium are our main specialisms, plus most other engines worth rebuilding. We offer a 3-month/10k-mile warranty.

Do you do MOTs?

No, we are a mechanical repair garage. We can rectify MOT failures and advisories so your car passes its next test.

Do you offer vehicle recovery?

Yes, we can arrange recovery from anywhere in the UK at customer cost.

Do you fit customer-supplied tyres?

Yes, we can fit tyres if you supply them. We do not stock or sell tyres.

Do you take trade work?

Yes, we work with used car dealers, recovery firms and warranty companies across the UK.

How long does an engine rebuild take?

Typically 1-3 weeks depending on parts availability and damage extent. We'll give you a clear timeframe before work starts.
