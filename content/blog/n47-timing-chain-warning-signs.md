## What customers usually notice

Common reported symptoms can include chain rattle, poor starting, warning lights, uneven running or a sudden non-start. Some symptoms can overlap with other faults, so the right first step is diagnosis rather than assuming the chain is the only issue.

## What the garage needs to confirm

A proper inspection may include fault code checks, live data, oil condition checks, noise assessment and mechanical testing where appropriate.

## When to contact Quick Solution Vehicles

If the car is noisy, non-running or has suspected chain damage, call Quick Solution Vehicles before continuing to drive it. Recovery can be arranged at customer cost.
